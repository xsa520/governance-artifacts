# Decision: Override Attempt Rejected

## Context
An attempt was made to override an existing governance constraint
due to perceived urgency or exceptional circumstances.

## Requested Action
Manual override of an enforced behavioral restriction.

## Governance Response
Request denied.

## Reason
The governance system does not permit discretionary overrides,
including emergency or exceptional conditions.

## Outcome
No action was taken.
The constraint remained enforced.

## Notes
This decision was recorded for audit completeness.
